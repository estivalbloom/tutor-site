Esti Niosi's Tutoring

Services

Hands on, one-on-one tutoring , either online or in-person, for computer science, mathematics, and physics courses. Esti teaches with a focus on guiding students towards understanding at their own pace, and ensuring that students feel that they're capable of solving problems themselves, rather than just being fed answers.

About Esti

Esti is a teacher with a long time passion for sharing her love of math. She believes that there's a beauty in understanding the world through logic that she wishes to share with others, and holds a firm belief that no one is really "bad at math" — it's just a difficult subject that's very punishing to fall behind on. Given some patience and guidance, anyone is capable of understanding it, and succeeding in their math courses!
